DNAnexus
DNAnexus is a cloud-based genome informatics & data management platform based in Mountain View, California. The platform allows for scalable analysis of raw sequencing data, as well as a medium for collaboration across scientists and organizations. In the summer of 2015, I had the opportunity to intern as a Software Development Intern in the Membrane team. I divided my internship between implementing features on the platform, and building an internal dashboard from scratch. The dashboard, which is used for a overview of both company and system health, allowed me to define, build, and refine a product from scratch. The project was successfully delivered at the end of the internship, and is to be used as a primary company health & system diagnostic tool at DNAnexus.
Technologies Knockout.js, Bootstrap, HTML, LESS, CoffeeScript, D3.js, jQuery
Prototyping: Adobe Illustrator, Keynote
Lessons First time building a product that people depend on daily
Conducted extensive user interviews
