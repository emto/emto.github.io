CMYK
CMYK is an annual designathon hosted by UC Berkeley's design group, Innovative Design. In Fall 2014, I was given the opportunity to organize this event, which involved meeting with professional designers and organizing panels that spanned from UX to visual branding. I had the privilege of working with designers from companies such as Turner Duckworth and Pixar, which made the publicity of the event all the more crucial. I contributed heavily to the event page of CMYK Designathon, which was used as the primary source of information for registration, information, and competition submissions.
